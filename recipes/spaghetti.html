Spaghetti

Description

Making spaghetti is simple and tasty. You cook pasta until it’s perfectly tender, then coat it in a rich, savory tomato sauce, with hints of garlic and herbs for added flavor. The result is a comforting, delicious dish where the sauce clings to each strand, making every bite satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until soft.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Toss spaghetti in sauce, then serve.
