Chickpea Salad with Avocado

Description

The Chickpea Salad with Avocado is a refreshing, simple dish that's perfect for a quick, healthy meal. The creamy avocado pairs beautifully with the crunchy cucumber and juicy tomatoes, while the chickpeas add a satisfying, protein-packed base. With just a few ingredients, this salad is not only delicious but also vegan-friendly, light, and full of vibrant flavors. It's ideal for lunch, as a side, or even a snack, and can be made in just a few minutes for a satisfying, wholesome bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 ripe avocado (diced)
  • 1 cucumber (diced)
  • 1 small red onion (thinly sliced)
  • A handful of cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh herbs (optional, like parsley or cilantro)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, diced avocado, cucumber, red onion, and cherry tomatoes.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice to taste.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then toss everything together gently to avoid mashing the avocado.
  4. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
  5. Serve immediately or chill in the fridge for 30 minutes for a refreshing, cool salad.
