Pizza

Description

Making pizza is simple and rewarding. You start with a soft, homemade dough that's topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Once baked, the pizza has a crispy, golden crust and a perfect balance of savory flavors, with the cheese melting into a deliciously satisfying bite.

The combination of crispy edges and tender center makes each slice a treat, whether you like it loaded with veggies, meats, or just a classic cheese topping.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix 2 cups flour, 1 packet yeast, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add 3/4 cup warm water and 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  3. Knead into dough and let rise for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough.
  5. Spread tomato sauce on top.
  6. Add cheese.
  7. Bake at 475°F (245°C) for 10-12 minutes.
